FAQ

Is Asterisk free? –

Yes—Asterisk is free on Mac. We may add optional paid features later, but writing and recording are included.

Are my recordings private? +

Voice capture and transcription stay on your Mac. Nothing is sent to the cloud without you choosing to share.

Can I export my writing? +

Yes. Copy, share, or export drafts anytime so your work is portable.

Does it work offline? +

Yes. Type or dictate without a connection. Your writing and recordings stay local; sync and share when you’re online.

How do I start dictation? +

Hit the record button or press the shortcut (⌘⇧R by default) to capture your voice and auto-transcribe into your draft.
