Privacy Policy

This policy explains how we handle your data for the Asterisk app and site. We keep collection minimal and only use data to deliver the service.

What we collect

  • Account and sign-in data from Apple (for authentication).
  • Basic device and app diagnostics for stability and performance.
  • Story progress and choices to sync your experience across devices.

How we use data

  • Provide and improve the app experience.
  • Support requests and account recovery.
  • Security, fraud prevention, and to meet legal requirements.

Storage & sharing

We do not sell your data. Limited processors (e.g., Apple services, analytics) may handle data under strict agreements. Data is stored securely and retained only as needed for the service.

Your choices

  • Request data export or deletion by emailing privacy@asterisk.app.
  • Adjust analytics/permissions in your device settings where available.
  • Stop using the app and request deletion to remove stored data.

Children

Asterisk is not directed to children under 13 (or lower age as required by local law). We do not knowingly collect data from children.

Changes

We may update this policy as the product evolves. We’ll post revisions here and update the effective date.

Contact

Questions or requests: privacy@asterisk.app.
